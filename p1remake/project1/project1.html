    Information

  • Put input into the box(K,C,Q,etc)
  • Remember, max 4 character per area
  • You can choose 10 of all character
  • Winner is the player who win more area
  • You can add unrelated letter to input to confuse your opponent
  • The letter must not be the symbol of characters to make the game fair
  • Good luck have fun


    • Scores

  • (K)ing= Quantity of allies in an area (1)
  • (Q)ueen= 5 (1)
  • (C)avalry= 10 times enemy quantity (1)
  • (A)ssassin=3;(autowin when 1 vs 1) (1)
  • (S)niper=3;(kill highest opponent point (1)
  • (P)awns=@1;(4 Pawns in an area will raise the points into 30 (4)
  • (R)ebel=3;(autowin vs royalty) (1)
  • (B)omber= -5 (win area when enemy has 3 or more warriors) (1)


    • Skills

  • King in presence with Queen make total points of 30
  • 4 Pawn gathers in 1 area make total point of 30
  • Assassin will perform instant kill when 1 vs 1 in an area
  • Sniper kill the highest point of character in area
  • Bomber will win the area if opponent has 3 or more quantities
  • Rebel will win the area if there is royalty such as King or Queen
